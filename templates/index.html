About Fidélité.ai

Welcome to Fidélité.ai, your partner in understanding and preventing customer churn. We leverage the power of artificial intelligence to help businesses across various sectors, including Banking, SaaS, and Telecom, identify potential churn risks and implement effective retention strategies.

Our platform offers two primary functionalities:

  • Predict Churn: Choose your sector and input customer details to receive a churn prediction and probability score.
  • Custom Analysis: Upload your own CSV data for a comprehensive AI-driven analysis of potential churn drivers and actionable preventive measures.

At Fidélité.ai, we believe that understanding customer behavior is the key to building lasting loyalty. Let us help you predict, prevent, and prosper.
